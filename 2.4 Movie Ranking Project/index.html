The Best Movies According to Jason

My top 3 movies of all-time

The Simpsons Movie

Released in 2007, The Simpsons Movie follows Homer’s monumental blunder of polluting Lake Springfield, forcing the family into exile after the EPA imprisons the town under a giant dome. Homer must redeem himself and save the town from destruction while navigating a tense family crisis. It's a high-stakes, satirical, and self-aware adventure.

Captain America: The First Avenger

Captain America: The First Avenger (2011) is a WWII-era Marvel superhero film exploring Steve Rogers’ (Chris Evans) journey from a sickly Brooklyn youth to a super-soldier after receiving an experimental serum. Rogers combats the villainous Red Skull (Hugo Weaving) to stop HYDRA from using the powerful Tesseract, upholding his moral duty.

Mad Max: Fury Road

Mad Max: Fury Road (2015) is a high-octane, post-apocalyptic masterpiece directed by George Miller, featuring a relentless, feature-length chase across a desolate wasteland. The film follows warrior Imperator Furiosa (Charlize Theron) as she rebels against the tyrannical Immortan Joe by rescuing his enslaved wives in a heavily armored "War Rig". Haunted survivor Max Rockatansky (Tom Hardy) reluctantly joins her, turning a desperate escape into a spectacular quest for redemption, survival, and freedom
